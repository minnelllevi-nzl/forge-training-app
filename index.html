Tactical conditioning

Session state Ready
  1. Loaded carry or bodyweight circuit to raise heart rate.
  2. Controlled recovery: eyes up, posture set, Equilibrium breathing under control.
  3. Move into a pressure tool or cognitive task while still under load.

ACE Cognitive Conditioning

A Awareness

Maintain 360-degree awareness of people, threats, opportunities, terrain, and changes in the environment.

C Composure

Eyes up, shoulders back, strong neutral posture. Project control even when tired or under pressure.

E Equilibrium

Regulate breathing to calm the nervous system, oxygenate the brain, and protect clear decisions.

Enter ACE mode

Run your ACE check: scan the environment before the pressure narrows your focus.

Action plan

Training outcomes

  • Explain Awareness, Composure, and Equilibrium as one pressure-control sequence.
  • Apply an ACE check in high-pressure scenarios.
  • Use posture, scan discipline, and Equilibrium breathing to regain control.
  • Self-assess reactions and create a personal response plan.
